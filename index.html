Nurturing Homes and the Planet Since 2012

EcoHaven was launched to bridge sustainable living with accessible design, sourcing from global cooperatives using non-toxic materials and low-energy processes. We prioritize products that last, reducing waste and promoting circular economy principles. Our team of eco-designers ensures every item meets rigorous standards for biodegradability and recyclability.

Through initiatives like product take-back programs and material innovation labs, we continually evolve to meet environmental challenges. We've diverted 500 tons of plastic from oceans and supported 200 artisan communities. Sustainable living is simple with EcoHaven—beautiful, functional, and earth-friendly.

Our impact reports detail carbon savings and biodiversity contributions, empowering you to make informed, positive choices for your home and the world.
